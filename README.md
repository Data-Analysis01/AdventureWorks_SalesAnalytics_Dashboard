# AdventureWorks Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates the development of a comprehensive Business Intelligence Dashboard using the AdventureWorks dataset in Power BI. The solution provides detailed insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional business trends through interactive visualizations and advanced analytical techniques.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across products, customers, and regions.
* Monitor business KPIs and profitability metrics.
* Provide dynamic and interactive reporting capabilities.
* Enable data-driven decision-making through actionable insights.

---

## Data Preparation

### Data Importing

* Imported multiple business datasets into Power BI.
* Consolidated and structured data for analytical reporting.

### Data Cleaning & Transformation

* Removed inconsistencies and duplicate records.
* Managed missing and null values.
* Standardized data formats and business attributes.
* Performed transformation using Power Query Editor.

---

## Data Modeling

Designed a Star Schema architecture consisting of:

### Fact Tables

* Fact Sales
* Fact Internet Sales
* Fact Reseller Sales

### Dimension Tables

* Dim Customer
* Dim Product
* Dim Date
* Dim Geography
* Dim Sales Territory

### Modeling Techniques

* Relationship Management
* Cardinality Optimization
* Performance-Oriented Data Modeling
* Business-Oriented Semantic Layer Design

---

## DAX Implementation

Developed advanced DAX calculations including:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Year-to-Date (YTD) Sales
* Quarter-to-Date (QTD) Sales
* Month-to-Date (MTD) Sales
* Previous Period Comparison
* Year-over-Year Growth Analysis
* Dynamic KPI Measures
